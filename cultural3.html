Analysis of Artifact

The set of the series is in an apocalyptic world. After a bio-chemical outbreak from a government facility, a disease spread. Once this viral disease gets into any animal, it shuts down their brain’s functions. The infected subject goes to a state that medical science referees as -‘legally dead’. Then the virus builds up in the subject. The subject becomes the medium of the virus to spread it to new nesting body. In the popular culture these infected subjects are called ‘Zombies’. But in this series, they are mentioned as ‘The walkers’ or ‘The dead’.

This series follows a group of people who are trying to survive this apocalypse. They are from different types of social backgrounds and work-place, such as- Policeman, Farmers, Doctors, Housewife, Engineers, etc. The group also consists with people from various age-group and race. All these people fused together to create a strong group, which helps them survive this bizarre world. Surviving alone in this apocalypse is impossible none the less. Some characters in this group are weak and disabled. They can’t live to continue their lives without the help of the group. But with the help form the group they are continuing to live their lives, and also contributing to the group. One of the examples of that would be the character named Hershel Greene‎, played by the actor Scott Wilson. He is a formal farmer who has some medical experiences. He lost his left leg in an attack by the walkersDespite ignoring him, they took good care of him. Despite not being able to contribute with physical strength, Hershel contributes with his medical knowledge.
