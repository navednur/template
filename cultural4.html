Description of Issue

This series highlights various social issues that we face day to day. One of those issues is- the lack of humanity in today’s world. Humanity is to help each-other in time of need. It is humanity that makes our group or society stronger. There are many people in the society that faces hard time to survive in this world. Many suffer from– Hunger, Depression, Diseases, Disabilities, etc. Many people don’t even have any place to live. Some of them have nothing to feed themselves and their families. They live their lives helpless. These people are a very important part of our society. They can contribute to the well-being of this society as much as any other people can, only if they are given a small chance.
